Official Rules

ELIGIBILITY: The Porsche Cayman/Wine Spectator sweepstakes begins May 28, 2013 and ends June 11, 2013. All attendees at Wine Spectator’s Bring Your Own Magnum Party in Sonoma are eligible to enter.

HOW TO ENTER: To enter, take a photograph with the 2014 Porsche Cayman located in the driveway at the Hotel Healdsburg, 25 Matheson St., Healdsburg, Calif. Tag your photo on Instagram or Twitter with #WSPorsche.

Photos will be uploaded to the BYOM.winespectator.com microsite, where all submitted photos can be viewed.

SELECTION OF WINNER: All submitted photos will be entered into a drawing on June 12, 2013; the winner will be picked at random by Wine Spectator magazine. Winner will be notified by Twitter or Instagram and on BYOM.winespectator.com.

PRIZES: Winner will receive (2) pieces of Driver’s Selection by Porsche Design luggage—AluFrame M Trolley—valued at $2,450 ($1,225 per piece), plus a Wine Spectator gift bag.

GENERAL TERMS AND CONDTIONS: Submission of an entry constitutes and signifies the entrant’s agreement and consent that Wine Spectator may use the entry, entrant’s name and photograph for promotional purposes and in all media worldwide.
